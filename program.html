Programme Highlights

A dynamic program featuring world-class keynotes, interactive workshops, policy discussions, and unmatched networking opportunities.

Keynote Lectures

Internationally renowned experts presenting the latest breakthroughs in molecular imaging research and clinical applications.

Scientific & Technical Sessions

In-depth sessions covering advances in PET, SPECT, MRI, optical imaging, theranostics, AI-driven imaging, and more.

Fireside Chats & Expert Panel Discussions

Candid, interactive conversations with leading scientists and clinicians on the future of precision medicine.

Practical, skills-focused workshops designed for students and early-career researchers.

Structured networking sessions connecting academia, industry, clinicians, and policymakers for meaningful collaborations.

A special tribute lecture honouring the pioneer of molecular imaging — a cornerstone of the conference celebrating his contributions to the field.
