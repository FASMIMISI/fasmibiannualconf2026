Why Sponsor

Exhibit or Sponsor at FASMI 2026

In an era where molecular imaging is revolutionizing precision medicine, sponsoring this groundbreaking conference offers unparalleled opportunities for visibility, networking, and thought leadership. As the first joint international conference of its kind in India, backed by prestigious global societies like FASMI and WMIS, it represents a historic milestone in advancing the field across Asia and beyond.

Highly Engaged, Multidisciplinary Global Audience: This targeted audience provides sponsors with direct access to key decision-makers, innovators, and end-users driving adoption in a rapidly growing market.

Unprecedented Visibility and Networking: With the endorsement and backing of renowned global societies, this event elevates molecular imaging on an international stage for the first time in India. The conference also enjoys high-level government support, including the expected presence of a senior cabinet member from the State of Telangana, underscoring its national significance and offering sponsors exceptional visibility at the highest echelons of policy and healthcare leadership.

By sponsoring, your brand aligns with innovation, global collaboration, and precision medicine's future — gaining prominent exposure through branding, speaking opportunities, exhibits, and networking in a truly international forum.
